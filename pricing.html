How Every Project Starts

Before any camera turns on, we understand your goal, your audience, and your constraints. Here's what working with us looks like from the first conversation to final delivery.

01

Discovery Call

We talk about what you're trying to accomplish, who you're trying to reach, and what success looks like for your organization. No scoping form, just a real conversation.

02

Strategy & Scope

We develop a communications strategy and a project scope together. You'll know exactly what we're producing, why, and how it connects to your goals before we agree to anything.

03

Production

We handle scripting, logistics, filming, and editing. One point of contact from start to finish. No handoffs between a strategy team and a production team — it's the same people.

04

Delivery & Review

You review the work with a clear revision process. We deliver final files formatted for every platform you need, and we stay available after delivery if something comes up.
