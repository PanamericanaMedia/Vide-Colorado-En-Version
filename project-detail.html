A Public Awareness Campaign, In English & Spanish

About the Project

This is a placeholder case study illustrating the kind of project Video Colorado is built for — replace with a real project once available.

"Building Trust" is a public awareness campaign produced for a Colorado government agency that needed to reach residents in both English and Spanish without the Spanish version feeling like an afterthought.

We started with a messaging strategy — what residents needed to know, what concerns they actually had, and what would make the message feel credible — before either version was scripted, so neither language was treated as a translation of the other.

Behind the Scenes

We filmed at locations the agency's own audience would recognize, rather than a generic studio backdrop, so the campaign felt like it was made for Colorado, not adapted for it. Both language versions were planned and shot together rather than one being an afterthought of the other.

Creative Direction

Tone mattered as much as accuracy. The English and Spanish scripts were written separately by people who think in each language, then reviewed together — not translated line by line — so both versions read as natural rather than as a copy of the other.
